We are a team of professionals bringing complementary skills and expertise from the fields of Finance, Technology, and Research aimed to provide an easy solution to keep hold of your receipts to combat the use of paper receipt.

About Us

Our story starts with our 1st Receipt Minder, Ariz Husain - a business professional with several years of experience in banking and retail industries, who witnessed the piles of paper receipts in the bins outside every retail business and the chaotic experience of managing paper receipts. His thoughts became nightmares when he studied the extent to which paper receipts can damage the environment. So, he pledged to build a simple and secure digital solution where consumers can receive their receipts, transaction data and proof of purchase from different businesses in a single mobile app while simultaneously contributing to the welfare of the environment. Later, Ariz was joined by two more Minders from the field of Data Analytics and Digital Technology to take this task forward. With ReceiptMinder, we are aiming to move towards a sustainable approach of data handling with the joint effort of both consumers and businesses.

We have put people on Mars, why can't we replace paper receipts with a digital solution? Yes, we can now.

If you are involved with paper receipts, become a Receipts Minder and help us to save the environment.
