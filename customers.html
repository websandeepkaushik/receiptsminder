Do your bit to protect the environment

11.3 Billion paper receipts are used annually in the UK only resulting in the cutting of 200,000 trees, emission of approx. 28,000 Tonnes of CO2 and 10,640 Tonnes of paper waste.

 

Saying no yes to ReceiptsMinder is one of the easiest ways that you can reduce your carbon footprint.

 

ReceiptsMinder helps to minimize the use of paper receipts, which usually contain bisphenol A (BPA) and bisphenol S (BPS). The absorption of bisphenol happens through the skin when touching the thermal paper receipts, and therefore affects cashier personnel as well as you.

Data Privacy

RM promises to keep private data securely on cloud and consumers expect transparent communication and intuitive consent options to give, review and withdraw their approval for processing their data.

For example, consumers might hesitate to insert their email address or mobile phone number at the point of sales. RM gives a unique identity number to each consumer, which can be used at a point of sales system. Email address or mobile phone will only be used to receive One-Time Passwords (OTPs) for registration and login.
